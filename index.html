Diving is a wonderful opportunity to see the undiscovered world below. While diving may not be for everyone, if you think you might be interested sign up for a class today via the link below!

    Why Dive:
  • Diving is theraputic
  • Diving can take you anywhere and every where in the world
  • Diving creates opportunities for you to meet new people
    Things to Consider:
  • Always start diving with a certified class
  • Ensure you use your own equipment or borrow from a trusted supplier
  • Always check dive tour reviews before participating
  • Dive with someone you trust (sometimes this means not diving with your spouse
